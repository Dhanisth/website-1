What is this website?

Firstly, a labor of love. Secondly, a place to showcase what I can do to potential employers. Thirdly, part of a friendly portfolio competition with my friend and peer, Max Farver. This website is built with Angular 8 and deployed using AWS S3 + Cloudfront

Why software development?

The moment that I made my first program is fixed in my mind. I wrote some code to check my answers to geometry problems in high school. I recall it like people remember their first kiss. The feeling of deep satisfaction was unmatched. Using only my brain and a computer, I was solving problems. The challenge of new and difficult concepts, fighting rogue semicolons, and syntax errors was all worth it. Eight years and one carrer choice later, I still get the same rush from code. I have never in my life found something that makes me feel as euphoric as making good code. I can't think of anything I'd rather do.
